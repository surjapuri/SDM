Pattern: Fake Work-From-Home Job Offer
Victims are offered a work-from-home job (often "product review" or "task
completion" gigs) via WhatsApp/Telegram, asked to pay a small refundable
"registration" or "task unlock" fee, and shown fake early payouts to build
trust before the amounts requested grow much larger with promises of
bigger payouts that never materialize. Legitimate employers never ask a
candidate to pay money to receive a job or complete work tasks.
