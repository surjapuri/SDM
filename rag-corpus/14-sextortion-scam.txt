Pattern: Sextortion / Blackmail Scam
A scammer initiates a video call (often via a fake profile), records or
screenshots compromising content, then threatens to share it with the
victim's contacts unless a payment is made, sometimes escalating demands
even after payment. Victims should not pay — payment does not guarantee
the content won't be shared and often leads to repeated demands — and
should instead preserve evidence and report to cybercrime.gov.in or 1930.
