Privacy Policy

QR scanning and offline message checks run entirely in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere.

The optional AI second opinion is off by default and sends only the text/image you explicitly submit to our server, which forwards it to Mesh API. It is not stored afterward.

Success stories you explicitly submit and consent to publish are stored in our database (success_stories/successStories). We ask you not to include anyone else's personal identifying details (phone numbers, account numbers, full addresses of third parties) in your story text — only your own experience.

A human reviews each story before it is ever published publicly or on social media.
