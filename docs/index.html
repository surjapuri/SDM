About QRaksha

India's free, multilingual cyber safety app

🛡️ What does QRaksha do?

QRaksha helps you check whether a QR code, URL, phone number, email address, or SMS sender is safe before you interact with it. It uses a three-tier analysis — fast offline heuristics → free threat-intel feeds → Advanced AI — and always shows the most cautious result.

📷 How does QR scanning work?

Your camera captures the QR image on-device using the Html5-QrCode library. The decoded text is analysed locally — no image is ever uploaded. The camera feed never leaves your phone.

🔗 How are suspicious links detected?

The risk engine checks URLs against known phishing/malware blocklists (PhishTank, OpenPhish, URLhaus), suspicious keyword patterns, URL-shortener detection, brand impersonation checks, and mismatched domain signals. Each check adds a risk score — the aggregate determines the final verdict.

💬 How is sensitive text scanned?

Phone numbers are checked against India-specific scam databases. Emails are pattern-analysed for spoofing. SMS headers are checked against TRAI sender ID patterns. All checks run offline first — AI is called only when needed, always through our secure server proxy, never directly from your browser.

⚙️ The Three-Tier Risk Engine

Tier 1 — Offline Heuristics

Instant. No network needed. Pattern matching against 500+ known scam signatures, URL structure checks, keyword analysis. Always available — even in airplane mode.

Tier 2 — Free Threat Intel

Checks live blocklists — PhishTank, OpenPhish, URLhaus — refreshed daily via GitHub Actions. No AI cost. No API key needed. Covers tens of thousands of known malicious URLs.

Tier 3 — Mesh Advanced AI ✨

Deep contextual analysis for ambiguous or complex cases. Powered by Mesh API — an advanced AI aggregator that combines the power of multiple world-class AI providers including ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), Gemini (Google), and many more, selecting the best model for each analysis. Runs through our Firebase Cloud Function — your data is proxied safely, never sent directly from your browser. The most cautious verdict from any tier always wins.

🔐 Safety & Privacy

QRaksha never stores your scans. Camera frames are processed locally and discarded. The only data sent to our servers is the specific text/URL you explicitly ask to check via AI — and only when you tap "Analyze with AI". We do not sell your data. Core safety features are permanently free.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect: QRaksha does not collect, store, or transmit your camera feed, scan history, or device ID. The only data ever sent to our servers is the specific URL/text you explicitly submit for AI analysis — and only when you tap "Analyze with AI".

AI analysis: When you use Mesh AI analysis, the text you submit is sent to our Firebase Cloud Function, which forwards it to the Mesh AI API. It is not logged, stored, or used for model training. We do not share it with any third party beyond the Mesh API call itself.

Firestore (community signatures): If you submit a scam report, the normalized value (URL/phone/email) and category are stored in Firestore to build a community blocklist. No personal information is stored alongside it.

Firebase Analytics: We do not use Firebase Analytics or any third-party tracking pixels.

Local storage: Your language preference and theme choice are saved to localStorage on your device only — not sent anywhere.

Cookies: We do not use advertising cookies. A consent notice is shown on first visit for the service worker (PWA) only.

Contact: For privacy concerns: imtiyazkth@gmail.com

QRaksha uses a service worker to work offline. No personal data is stored or shared.
