Pattern: Fake Electricity Bill Disconnection Notice
An SMS claims the recipient's electricity connection will be disconnected
that same night due to an unpaid bill, providing a phone number to call
"customer support" immediately. The scammer on the other end guides the
victim to install a remote-access app or make a payment to a personal UPI
ID. Electricity providers do not threaten same-day disconnection via SMS
with a personal callback number, and never ask for remote-desktop access
to resolve a billing issue.
