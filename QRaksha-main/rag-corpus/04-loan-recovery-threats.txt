Pattern: Loan/EMI Recovery Threats
Victims (sometimes people who never took a loan, or who used an unlicensed
instant-loan app) receive threatening calls or messages claiming an EMI is
overdue and that legal action, arrest, or workplace/family shaming will
follow unless immediate payment is made. Unlicensed loan apps sometimes
combine this with threats to misuse photos or contacts harvested from the
victim's phone. Legitimate lenders follow RBI-mandated recovery processes
and do not threaten instant arrest or family shaming to recover a debt.
