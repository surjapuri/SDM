Pattern: Staged Uniformed Video Call
As part of digital-arrest and impersonation scams, fraudsters set up a
video call background resembling a police station or government office,
sometimes wearing a uniform, to appear more convincing while pressuring
the victim for money or personal information. Genuine law-enforcement
interactions in India do not happen this way — legitimate summons or
investigation steps arrive through official written notices and in-person
or court process, never through an unsolicited video call.
