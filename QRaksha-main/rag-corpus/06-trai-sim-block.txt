Pattern: Fake TRAI / SIM Deactivation Notice
Victims receive an automated call or message claiming to be from TRAI
(Telecom Regulatory Authority of India), stating their SIM card will be
deactivated within hours due to "misuse" or a pending complaint, and are
asked to press a number or call back to resolve it — leading into a
scam-call funnel that often escalates into a digital-arrest scenario.
TRAI does not deactivate individual SIM cards and does not contact
subscribers this way; SIM issues are handled solely through the telecom
operator via verified channels.
