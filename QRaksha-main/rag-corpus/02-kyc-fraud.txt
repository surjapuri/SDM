Pattern: Fake KYC Update Fraud
Victims receive an SMS or WhatsApp message claiming their bank account,
SIM card, or e-wallet KYC (Know Your Customer) details will expire or be
suspended within 24 hours unless they click a link and "update" their
details. The link leads to a fake bank/telecom login page designed to
steal banking credentials, or asks the victim to install a remote-access
app. Real banks and telecom companies do not send KYC-update deadlines via
unsolicited SMS links, and never ask customers to install remote-access
software to complete KYC.
