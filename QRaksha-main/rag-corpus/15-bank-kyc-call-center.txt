Pattern: Fake Bank Customer Care Number
Victims search online for a bank's customer care number and find a fake
number (often placed via fraudulent listings or comments) instead of the
real one, then call it believing it's official support. The scammer
answers pretending to be bank staff, and proceeds with an OTP-sharing or
remote-access request to drain the account. Always use the number printed
on the back of a bank card or the bank's official app/website, never a
number found through a general web search.
