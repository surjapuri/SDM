Pattern: Romance / Matrimonial Investment Scam
A scammer builds a relationship over weeks on a matrimonial or dating
platform, eventually introducing an "investment opportunity" (often crypto
or a trading app) and encouraging the victim to invest increasing amounts,
sometimes showing fabricated profits before disappearing once large sums
are transferred. A request for financial investment or money transfer
arising from an online relationship, especially one that escalates in
urgency, is a well-documented fraud pattern regardless of how genuine the
relationship feels.
