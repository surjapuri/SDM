Pattern: OTP Sharing & Remote-Access App Requests
A caller poses as a bank representative, telecom support agent, or
"refund processing" agent and asks the victim to share an OTP received by
SMS, or to install a remote-access app (AnyDesk, TeamViewer, QuickSupport)
so the "agent" can help resolve an issue. Once installed, the scammer can
see the victim's screen and initiate transactions. No legitimate bank,
telecom company, or government agency will ever ask a customer to share an
OTP or install remote-access software over an unsolicited call.
