Pattern: Fake Lottery / Prize Winning Scam
Victims are told via SMS, call, or email that they have won a lottery,
lucky draw, or prize from a well-known brand, and must pay a "processing
fee," "customs duty," or "tax" upfront to release the winnings. No
legitimate lottery or prize requires the winner to pay money upfront to
receive it — this is a payment-extraction scam using excitement as the
pressure tactic.
